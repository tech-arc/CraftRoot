Why Cut the Middleman?

In traditional retail, multiple layers of distributors, wholesalers, and physical retailers inflate the price of a product by up to 60-80%.

By using our direct-to-consumer platform, manufacturers earn a fairer margin for their hard work, and you get premium products delivered safely at genuine factory prices.
